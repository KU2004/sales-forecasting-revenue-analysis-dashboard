# Sales Forecasting & Revenue Trend Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on forecasting future revenue using historical sales data and analyzing business growth trends.

---

## Business Problem

Business leaders need accurate forecasts to:

- Plan budgets
- Manage inventory
- Allocate resources
- Achieve revenue targets

---

## Objective

Develop a forecasting dashboard for revenue planning and performance monitoring.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Excel
- Forecasting Analysis
- Revenue Analytics

---

## KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Revenue Growth %
- Forecast Accuracy %
- Profit Margin %
- Target Achievement %

---

## Dashboard Features

### Revenue Trend Analysis
Monthly revenue tracking.

### Forecast vs Actual Analysis
Forecast accuracy measurement.

### Regional Revenue Analysis
Performance by geography.

### Product Performance Dashboard
Revenue by category.

### Profitability Analysis
Margin comparison.

---

## Key Insights

- High-growth regions
- Revenue trends
- Forecasting gaps
- Product growth opportunities

---

## Recommendations

- Improve forecast accuracy
- Increase focus on profitable products
- Optimize sales strategy

---

## Business Impact

Enables data-driven financial planning and revenue forecasting.
